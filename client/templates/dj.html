muse

Keep in mind, I have only tried mixing twice in my life (and I loved it).

But I always listen to Boiler Rooms, live sets, or festival music whenever I study.

Plus, it has always been one of my dreams to be a producer/DJ/or a contributor to the music industry.

With that being said, MUSE is my "first contribution" to this industry.

how it works

  1. A user enters their SoundCloud account name into the search bar.
  2. The application begins to scrape the users' liked songs, as you can connect SoundCloud+ to software like Rekordbox and Serato Pro.
  3. After scraping and embedding the users' liked songs, we apply FAISS embeddings to embed the songs in a 3D space.
  4. The DJ can see the songs in this embedded space to find potential tracks to mix by searching the table or clicking in the space.
